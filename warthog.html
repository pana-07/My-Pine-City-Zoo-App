Warthog

Warthogs are wild pigs with bumpy "warts" on their faces and pointy tusks. They live in Africa and kneel on their front knees to munch grass! When scared, they zoom away with tails straight up like flags.

Question: How do warthogs eat grass close to the ground?

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?
