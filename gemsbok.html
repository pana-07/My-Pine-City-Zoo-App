Gemsbok

Gemsbok are beautiful antelopes with long, straight horns like swords!

They live in Africa's deserts and have special gray-brown fur with black-and-white patterns. Their amazing horns help them stay safe from predators, and they can survive without water for weeks by eating juicy plants.
