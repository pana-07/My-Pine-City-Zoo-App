Gemsbok

Gemsboks are small, agile antelopes found in southern Africa. They are known for their distinctive white markings and their ability to survive in arid environments.

They are herbivores, feeding primarily on grasses and leaves. Gemsboks are well-adapted to dry conditions and can go for long periods without water.
