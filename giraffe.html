ANIMALS MAP PLACES

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest animals on Earth, with super-long necks that let them munch leaves from treetops. They have spotted coats like puzzles and blue tongues as long as your arm! Baby giraffes can stand up just one hour after they're born. Question: Why do giraffes have such long necks?

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the giraffes at G15, just beyond the research centre.
