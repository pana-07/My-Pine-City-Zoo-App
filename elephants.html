elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong memory, and large ears.

Elephants are herbivores, meaning they eat only plants. Their diet includes grass, leaves, fruits, and bark. They use their trunks to grab food and bring it to their mouths.
